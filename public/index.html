How we met

Royce and Alexandra met in a court house, but neither of them were on trial. The court house was the setting for the first big social event of business school, and the theme was casino night. Alexandra was by the craps table, and Royce asked her to teach him how to play. Alexandra, having never played in her life, gave it her best shot. They didn’t win craps that night, but being the complete nerds that they are, they did talk about their shared background in healthcare, and about joining the healthcare club. By a stroke of luck, they both end up on the same healthcare club committee, and see each other at weekly meetings.

Fast forward to the second semester of business school, and Royce asks Alexandra to brunch. They bond over chocolate covered waffles and an art event before he takes her to the hottest date spot around — a counter-serivce thai restaurant. Despite the sparse surroundings, Royce wins over Alexandra’s heart, and the rest is history.

Join Us!

The nearest airport to the festivities is Detroit Metropolitan Airport (DTW). It’s a hub for Delta, so they’re the most likely to have direct flights. A taxi ride from the airport to the hotel is ~$50. There is also a shuttle service called Ride Skoot Shuttle which you can book for $43 round trip, $23 one way. Link to book the shuttle is here: https://www.rideskoot.com/. If you choose to rent a car, parking at the hotel is $35/day.

We’ve booked a block of hotel rooms at the Detroit Marriott in the Detroit Renaissance Center for wedding guests. The link to book online is here: https://resweb.passkey.com/go/dicksonchengwedding

The room rate is $129 per night, and you must reserve by August 26th.

Although there are other hotels in the Detroit area, we highly recommend staying at the Detroit Marriott because while we promise that Detroit is better than everyone says, there are still some areas that you don’t want to be staying!

We will have a shuttle service from the Marriott to the wedding and back to the hotel after the wedding. Details to come on timing.
